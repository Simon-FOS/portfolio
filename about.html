My Journey

From Administration to Web Development

My journey into technology began in 2017 when I commissioned SmartWeb Nigeria LTD to build a School Management System for a College, where I served as Principal. That project ignited a passion for programming, leading me to enroll with nHub Jos, Buildspace, Greg & Co, and other tech hubs across Nigeria.

Today, I am a Lead Developer and a Tech Educator with Sidasoft Technologies. My background in Philosophy and Educational Management gives me a unique perspective, blending analytical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving into every digital project I undertake.

I am committed to mastering both the technical and creative aspects of web development, ensuring that every website, application, or digital solution I deliver is functional, scalable, and provides an exceptional user experience.
